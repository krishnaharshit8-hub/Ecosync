**Problem Statement**:
Rapid urbanization and the shift to renewable energy have created a "Grid Instability Crisis." Centralized power grids struggle to balance intermittent renewable sources (solar/wind) with fluctuating urban demand, leading to energy waste, blackouts, and high costs. Furthermore, current smart meters are vulnerable to cyber-attacks, and energy distribution lacks transparency.
